## Why LIC (strategic rationale)

1. **Proven pattern**: Astoria indexed in 1-2 days with 3 impressions on
   commercial queries. East Village sits at pos 10 after ~4 days. The
   neighborhood-page template is earning faster indexing than blog posts.
2. **Distinct audience**: LIC targets higher-income renters (40x income
   needed ~$128k+ for 1BR) looking for luxury towers and amenities. None
   of our other neighborhood pages address this segment.
3. **Commercial intent**: "LIC apartments", "Long Island City rent",
   "Court Square apartments", "LIC no fee apartments" are all high-intent
   queries with weaker content in the top 10 compared to informational queries.
4. **Topical bridge**: LIC neighbors Astoria (directly), Williamsburg
   (visible across East River), Midtown East (1 subway stop). It connects
   our existing neighborhood cluster to Manhattan-alternative searches.
5. **Content hook**: Net effective vs gross rent + LIC concessions make a
   natural bridge to the new /best-time-to-rent-nyc pillar (Session 9's
   big bet). Cross-link reinforces the seasonal pillar's relevance.
